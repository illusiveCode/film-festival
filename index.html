Boston Film Festival

Welcome to Boston Film Festival!

The wait is over! August 5th to 8th in Boston Public Garden
Welcome to 'Watch it outside Boston edition' film festival. If you are looking for a fun and exciting day out then this festival is for you. It will be taking place from August 5th to 8th in a park called 'Boston Public Garden' and can hold up to 1,500 people. There are few movies to choose from, such as, Avengers: Infinity War, Grown Ups, Toy Story 2 and more. Don't miss this oppertunity so book your ticket(s) now! Enjoy the festival that has been taking place every year for the past 10 years!
